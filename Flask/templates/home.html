Gross National Income (GNI) per capita (PPP $) reflects the standard of living.

The Human Development Index was created by the United Nations Development Programme to emphasize that people and their capabilities are the ultimate criteria for assessing a country's development — not economic growth alone. It combines health, education, and income into a single composite score between 0 and 1.
